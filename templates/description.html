Overview

In this use case you have to solve a machine learning problem applied to the utilities world.

Use Case Description

You have a sample of data from electricity metrics of a 25 of condos in Ecuador available. Data are detected every 15 minutes and there are two years of historical depth available. SDG will guide you through the main phases of a company project, from data mapping to data exploration, from forecast to results' interpretation. We will understand together how to apply theoretical knowledge to specific busisess requirements.

Goal

The goal of this project is to forecast the electricity demand for one week, with 15 minutes frequency.

Data

Data are available at this link https://github.com/noemi-galli-sdg/SDG-Challenge , the file with training data is data_history.csv, download it and start your challenge.
